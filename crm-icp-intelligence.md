## 🛠️ Project 3: Automated Deal Intelligence & Predictive ICP Mapping

### 🔴 The Operational Bottleneck
The business development team held an active database of 190 high-potential company contacts inside the corporate CRM. However, every single profile lacked critical qualifying metadata required for strategic prioritization—specifically **Firm Type** and **Assets Under Management (AUM)**. 

Executing manual open-source intelligence (OSINT) research across 190 deep corporate entities would normally consume weeks of human resource hours, completely stalling outbound sales velocity. Furthermore, the team lacked an objective, standardized method to map these companies against our internal Ideal Customer Profile (ICP), resulting in inefficient, unprioritized team outreach.

### ⚙️ The Solution (Context-Aware LLM Research & Scoring Arrays)
Instead of manual hunting, I engineered a highly structured market analysis pipeline using the Claude LLM framework. I fed the system our internal proprietary company playbook along with the unrefined 190-contact CRM export.

[ 190 Cold CRM Profiles ] + [ Corporate Product Playbook ]│▼[ Claude LLM Analytics Engine ]│┌───────────────────┼───────────────────┐▼                   ▼                   ▼[ Firm Type & AUM ] [ 1-5 Product Fit ] [ Pain Points & Logic ]│                   │                   │└───────────────────┼───────────────────┘▼[ Structured Warm Lead Pipeline ]
* **Playbook Contextualization:** Uploaded our internal product playbook to anchor the LLM's logic strictly within our company's true definitions of target buyers.
* **Algorithmic Parameter Extraction:** Commanded the AI framework to act as an institutional investment researcher to find and extract Firm Type and AUM brackets for all 190 targets.
* **Predictive Fit Calculation:** Engineered a custom matrix instruction that evaluated the financial parameters and scored each company's product alignment on a $1_{\text{Lowest}} - 5_{\text{Highest}}$ scale.
* **Pain Point Deconstruction:** Directed the model to extract likely organizational pain points for each entity and supply a rigorous, logical text justification for the assigned fit rating.

### 📈 Measurable Business Impact
* **Pipeline Acceleration:** Transformed a tedious, multi-week market analysis task into a structured data asset completed in minutes.
* **Lead Transformation:** Successfully converted an unvalidated cold database into a **prioritized, high-value warm outreach queue** categorized cleanly by total asset values.
* **Strategic Personalization:** Armed Business Development Representatives (BDs) with immediate visibility into each prospect’s specific corporate bottlenecks, reducing cold-outreach friction and boosting response conversion.
