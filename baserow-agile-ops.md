## 🛠️ Project 1: Multi-Brand Knowledge Base Migration & Custom Training Architecture

### 🔴 The Operational Bottleneck
The company's core internal training base ("Outline") lacked centralized data and had no built-in features to test or grade the technical knowledge of Business Development Representatives (BDs). Data was scattered across complex, raw HTML source files across 8 separate corporate brands. Additionally, there was no way to automatically track who completed assessment modules or view results without manual tracking.

### ⚙️ The Solution (Data Migration & Automated Logic Engines)
I executed a comprehensive, multi-phase systems migration and built an interactive testing layer from scratch.

[ Raw HTML Sources (8 Brands) ] ──> [ Centralized Outline Migration ]│▼[ Embedded Testing Layer ]│┌───────────────────────┴───────────────────────┐▼     
▼(Phase 1: Microsoft Hyperlinks)                 (Phase 2: Baserow Upgrade)│▼[ Advanced Custom Formulas ]│▼[ Automated Webhook Alerts ](Instant Email)

1. **Precision Migration:** Extracted and parsed data out of raw HTML sources into Outline for 8 core brands, manually auditing every single hyperlinks and structural layout node to maximize user experience for reps.
2. **Assessment Integration (Phase 1):** Hyper-engineered a testing pipeline directly into Outline by embedding custom Microsoft Quiz links.
3. **Infrastructure Scaling (Phase 2):** Upgraded the back-end infrastructure to **Baserow**. Taught myself advanced Baserow relational formulas to programmatically capture, aggregate, and calculate quiz responses in real-time.
4. **Automated Notification Triggers:** Configured instant webhook communication relays so that the exact second a team member submitted a quiz, an automated email notification was fired straight to the designated quiz owner.

### 📈 Measurable Business Impact
* **Accessibility:** Unified data across 8 distinct brands into one streamlined hub, cutting team search times down significantly.
* **Automation:** Swapped out hours of manual quiz grading for instant, formula-calculated response arrays.
* **Knowledge Management:** Authored a comprehensive structural handover blueprint in Jira, allowing incoming technical squads to take over operations with zero friction.

## 🛠️ Project 2: Agile Project Governance & Pod Operations Leadership

### 🔴 The Operational Bottleneck
Upon moving to Business Operations (BizOps), I stepped into the role of Assistant Pod Manager. In a fast-paced environment, cross-functional tasks were at high risk of slipping through the cracks. The team needed rigid operational discipline, clear milestone visibility, and daily synchronization to hit tight company deliverable goals.

### ⚙️ The Solution (Agile Framework Governance)
I partnered directly with the Pod Manager to operationalize and enforce daily delivery standards.

* **Sprint Maintenance:** Leveraged **GitLab and Jira** as single sources of truth to map daily task assignments and resolve workflow bottlenecks.
* **Accountability Loops:** Audited and enforced the daily tracking of **Most Important Tasks (MITs)** for all team members on GitLab.
* **Asynchronous Coordination:** Championed and executed precise, time-sensitive end-of-day sprint recaps, making sure every blocker was surface-mapped and immediately addressed.

### 📈 Measurable Business Impact
* **Zero Drop Rate:** Achieved a perfect operational execution record where zero high-priority tasks fell through the cracks.
* **Velocity:** Drastically improved the speed of daily pod deliverables by introducing strict daily standup and recap guidelines.
* **Leadership Transition:** Successfully bridged technical architecture design (Biz Tech) with high-stakes team people management (BizOps).
